Autoplay Video on Scroll

This feature, programmed by Mohammad Sajjad Baqri allows you to autoplay videos on the screen of your device as you keep scrolling down. In the windows with small screen size, the videos will autoplay as you keep scrolling down. However, in windows with larger screen size, the video will only play only if you hover over the specific video.

Given credit to the programmer is necessary if you are using this feature in your website.
